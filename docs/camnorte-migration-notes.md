# BetterCamNorte Migration Notes

This repo has been mechanically rebranded from the BetterSolano source to BetterCamNorte. Basic Camarines Norte province data and official names have been wired in, but many service/detail pages still contain mock or mechanically renamed content.

Replace next:

- Officials: department heads, official photos, direct office contacts, and committee assignments.
- Hotlines and offices: emergency numbers, office emails, addresses, office-specific social links, and office hours.
- Services: municipal service pages still mirror the original structure; decide which provincial services stay and which pages should be removed.
- Legislative data: ordinances and resolutions are mock-renamed source records, not Camarines Norte records.
- Transparency data: budgets, competitive index, DPWH projects, and fiscal reports need Camarines Norte source datasets.
- External URLs: Filipizen, OASYS, official government sites, quiz links, and PDFs are placeholders until confirmed.
- Copy and translations: English, Filipino, and Ilocano strings were mechanically renamed and need human review.
