# Scope narrowed to a transparency watchdog

BetterCamNorte's scope is narrowed to its core mission — showing where Camarines Norte's public
money goes and who is accountable for it (see the *transparency watchdog* principle). Against that
test we are:

- **Keeping as core** (primary navigation): budget & fiscal transparency, DPWH / projects,
  officials, legislative acts (ordinances, resolutions).
- **Demoting to supporting** (out of primary nav, kept lean): statistics / demographics /
  competitive-index as context for judging spending; news, narrowed to budget- and
  project-accountability items.
- **Removing**: the services section and its ~24 service-detail pages (linking out to the official
  LGU service pages instead), the weather widget (Open-Meteo), and the currency converter
  (ExchangeRate).

We accept that this drops the "services" function the original README led with — the largest single
content area — because a focused watchdog is more credible, and more maintainable by a volunteer
team, than a do-everything portal.

**Consequences:** the README mission statement, the primary navigation, and internal links must be
updated; and the services "link out" must point at a real official URL, or the cut degrades to a
dead end.
