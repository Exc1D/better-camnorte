# BetterCamNorte

A public-funds transparency watchdog for the provincial government of Camarines Norte: it shows
where public money goes and who is accountable for it.

## Language

**Transparency watchdog**:
The site's identity and the test every section must pass — does it help a citizen see, understand,
or question how public funds are used, or who is accountable for them?
_Avoid_: portal, one-stop shop

**Core / Supporting / Boilerplate**:
The three scope tiers a section can fall into. _Core_ follows the money and earns primary navigation
(budget, projects, officials, legislative). _Supporting_ is context only — kept lean and out of
primary nav (statistics, news). _Boilerplate_ is unavoidable table stakes (contact, privacy, terms,
FAQ, accessibility).

**Provenance**:
The official source and effective date (`as_of`) attached to every published figure. A figure
without provenance does not ship.
_Avoid_: citation, reference
